About Me

Hi, I’m Dori—an Egyptologist and storyteller with a deep love for ancient worlds and the people who lived in them. I hold a PhD in Egyptology and have spent years working on projects that bring the past to life—from translating and conserving ancient papyri to reconstructing sacred texts in the Valley of the Kings. Through Dori of the Nile, I share stories that make Ancient Egypt feel vivid, beautiful, and deeply human. Whether it’s a whispered love letter from thousands of years ago or the legacy of a forgotten pharaoh, my goal is to spark curiosity, wonder, and a sense of connection between then and now.
